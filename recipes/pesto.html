Pesto

Description

Traditional pesto is a blend of fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, extra-virgin olive oil, and Parmesan cheese.

With a food processor, it comes together in just a few steps!

Ingredients

  • Nuts
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Pulse the nuts, lemon juice, and garlic in the food processor until they are finely chopped
  2. Add the basil and pulse again
  3. Next, drizzle in the olive oil with the food processor blade still running
  4. Finally, add grated Parmesan cheese and process briefly until combined
