Lasagna

Description

One of our top-performing recipes of all time! Our lasagna recipe has ranked among the most popular lasagna recipes on the internet for two decades.

Make our most popular lasagna today to find out what all the fuss is about!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Eggs (the secret ingredient)

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
